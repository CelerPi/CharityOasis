# CharityOasis White Paper

## Introduction

CharityOasis is a cryptocurrency designed to enhance transparency, reduce transaction costs, and build donor trust to promote global philanthropy. Our token (CHAT) will serve as the primary transaction medium and incentive mechanism to achieve these goals. Unlike traditional charity platforms, CharityOasis provides tangible incentives to donors, ensuring exponential growth in donor participation.

## Background

In an era where technological advancements continually reshape societal norms, blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies are becoming increasingly important in the charity sector. According to World Bank data, global charitable donations have been declining in recent years, mainly due to a lack of transparency and high transaction costs. Blockchain technology can address these issues with its immutability and public transparency.

### Integration with Physical Goods and NFTs

- **Online Store**:
  - **Physical Clothing Sales**: Sell physical clothing items in the online store, such as shirts, pants, shoes, hats, necklaces, and rings.
  - **Corresponding NFTs**: Users receive NFTs corresponding to the purchased physical clothing items, which can be used to decorate their virtual avatars.
- **Virtual Avatars and NFT Equipment**
  - **Avatar Creation**: Each user has a virtual avatar on the platform, which can be customized with NFT items.
  - **NFT Items**: NFTs obtained through purchasing physical goods can be equipped on virtual avatars, enhancing their appearance and rarity score. Each item has a different rarity score, categorized as common, rare, epic, or legendary.
  - **Equipment System**: Users can equip their avatars with NFT items to enhance their appearance and rarity score. All NFT items are detachable, allowing users to change and rearrange them at any time.
  - **Rarity Scoring System**: The total rarity score is calculated based on the equipped NFT items. The combined rarity scores of all items form the user's total score.
  - **Leaderboard System**: Weekly, monthly, and annual leaderboards will rank users based on their total rarity scores. Top-ranked users will receive different levels of token rewards.
  - **Points Accumulation**: Users earn points for purchasing physical items using tokens. The more they spend, the more points they earn.
  - **Points Redemption**: Users can redeem points for tokens or exclusive items in the store.

- **Create an NFT Marketplace**: Users can buy and sell NFTs in the platform's NFT marketplace. The platform charges a transaction fee, generating additional revenue.

### Holding Reward Mechanism

CHAT’s holding reward mechanism incentivizes users to hold tokens long-term, enhancing token stability and user participation. The mechanism takes daily snapshots of user holdings and distributes rewards proportionately. Holding rewards are calculated daily and distributed periodically (e.g., daily or weekly).

#### Advantages of the Holding Reward Mechanism

1. **Encourages Long-term Holding**: Daily reward distribution incentivizes users to hold tokens long-term, reducing short-term speculative behavior.
2. **Market Stability**: Gradual reward distribution reduces market volatility from large token releases, maintaining relative price stability.
3. **Fairness**: Dynamic reward adjustment based on actual holdings ensures fair and reasonable reward distribution.

#### Reward Calculation and Distribution

1. **Snapshot Mechanism**: Daily snapshots record user holdings.
2. **Reward Calculation**: Daily reward rate = annual interest rate / 365. For example, an initial annual interest rate of 10% results in a daily reward rate of 10% / 365 ≈ 0.0274%.
3. **Periodic Distribution**: Rewards are distributed daily or weekly, with users able to check and claim their rewards at any time.

#### Implementation Steps

1. **Set Reward Rate**: Initial annual interest rate is 10%, decreasing by 1% annually to a minimum of 5%. Daily reward rate = annual interest rate / 365.
2. **Deploy Smart Contract**: Develop and deploy smart contracts for snapshots, reward calculation, and distribution.
3. **Reward Distribution**: Distribute rewards periodically (e.g., daily or weekly), with users able to claim rewards anytime.
